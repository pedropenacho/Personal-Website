About Me

I am a young self-taught developer with strong problem-solving skills who always enjoyed to build things from scratch.
I was introduced to programming for the first-time during university but it was in my first job that I really got serious about coding and developed a strong passion for it. I was amazed with all the things I could do and create through coding. I had found something that allowed me to build useful products from nothing and with few resources through problem-solving and creativity, it almost looked like magic. I enjoyed so much the experience that I decided I wanted a career in IT.
That’s how my journey began.

Why Founders and Coders

I found Founders and Coders a few months ago when I was just starting learning web development by myself and I became immediately interested in your course. Learning with people who share the same passion for coding, and from experience developers, all of this for free!? I couldn’t even believe, it sounded too good to be true. Since then I have been wishing to be part of this programme, but I didn’t think I had the required skills to be consider for this course, thought I didn’t stand a chance and just postponed my application. Now after months of learning web development and with some personal projects on my portfolio, I’ve come a long way and believe to have what it takes to be part of Founders and Coders.
I finally decided to give it a go and apply for the next Founders and Coders cohort.
